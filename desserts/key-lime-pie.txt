Molet Family Key Lime Pie
-------------------------
Graham Cracker Crumbs
Butter
Sugar
1 can sweetened condensed milk
1 egg yolk
Juice of 3 limes

Make graham crumb crust according to box directions.  Bake about 5 minutes in pie pan.  Combine the milk with egg yolk and stir.  Add the lime juice gradually and stir.  Pour into pie crust and bake another 8 minutes at 350 degrees F.  Cool and refrigerate for at least 6 hours.  Serve with homemade whipped cream.
