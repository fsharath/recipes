Guacamole
---------
1/2 small onion, finely chopped
2 serrano chiles or 1 jalapeno chile, stemmed, seeded, and finely chopped
1 ripe, medium-large tomato, finely chopped
1 clove garlic, finely chopped
10 sprigs chopped coriander (optional)
3 ripe, medium avocados (Florida, not California)
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 lime, juiced

Mix all ingredients together.
