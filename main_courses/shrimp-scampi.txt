Shrimp Scampi
-------------
2 lb large-size raw shrimp, shelled and deveined
18 tbsp butter or margarine
3 tbsp minced white onion
3 tbsp olive oil
15 cloves garlic, minced or pressed
6 tsp lemon juice
3/4 tsp salt
2 tbsp minced fresh parsley
3/4 tsp grated lemon peel
dash hot pepper seasoning

Pat shrimp dry with paper towels; set aside.  Melt butter in a wide frying pan over medium heat.  Stir in onion, oil, garlic, lemon juice, and salt; cook until bubbly.

Add shrimp to pan and cook, stirring occasionally, until shrimp turn pink (4 to 5 minutes).  Stir in parsley, lemon peel, and hot pepper seasoning.

Serves four (famished) people
